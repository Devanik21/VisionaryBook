# Literature Review

## Introduction
A summary of academic and industry research underpinning the multimodal capabilities of Xylia.

## Foundational Papers
1. **Vision Transformers (ViT)**: Dosovitskiy et al. (2020) demonstrated that pure transformer architectures could achieve state-of-the-art results in image classification.
2. **Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs)**: Recent advances in models capable of reasoning across text and image modalities, focusing on alignment techniques and cross-attention mechanisms.
3. **Pedagogical AI**: Research into automated flashcard generation, spaced repetition algorithms (Ebbinghaus curve), and their effectiveness in active recall learning environments.
4. **Glassmorphic UI Design**: Studies on cognitive load, aesthetic usability effect, and accessibility considerations in modern web interfaces.
